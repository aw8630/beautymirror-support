# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2026

## 1. Information Collection
**BeautyMirror Pro** is designed with your privacy in mind. 
- **No Data Uploads:** All face detection, feature tracking (via Vision framework), and image rendering (via Metal) are performed strictly **offline** and **locally** on your device. 
- **No Personal Data Collected:** We do not collect, store, upload, or share your photos, facial data, or any other personal information with our servers or any third parties.

## 2. TrueDepth API / Vision Framework Usage
Our app uses Apple's native Vision framework to detect facial landmarks (such as eyes, nose, and lips) solely for the purpose of applying real-time cosmetic effects and portrait reshaping. 
This data is processed in real-time in the device's memory and is instantly discarded after the frame is rendered. It is never saved to a file or transmitted off your device.

## 3. Photo Library Access
The app requests access to your Photo Library only to let you select photos for editing and to save your edited masterpieces. We do not access or scan your library in the background.
